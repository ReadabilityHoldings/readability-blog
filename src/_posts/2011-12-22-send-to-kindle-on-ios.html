--- layout: post title: Send to Kindle... on iOS categories: - News - Release Notes tags: [] status: private type: post published: false meta: _edit_last: "14" --- A recent update to the iOS Kindle app extends Amazon's "Personal Documents" service beyond Amazon devices: you can now send any article from Readability to your iPhone, iPad, or iPad touch. To start receiving articles from the web:
  • download the Kindle app (or get the update)
  • choose an @kindle.com email address for your device
  • add a "Send to Kindle" addon or bookmarklet to all your favorite browsers
  • add your Kindle email address to your Readability settings
