--- layout: post title: Join us at SXSW categories: - News tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: "5" _wp_old_slug: "" --- If you're attending SXSW Interactive this year, Readability and Arc90 will have a small contingency roaming the streets of Austin. We've created an @arc90/SXSW on Twitter to make it easier to reach us. As for the conference itself, we're involved in a few of the talks:
  • On Saturday at 11am, I'll be joining a panel on rewarding creators and crowdfunding.
  • On Sunday at 12:30pm, we plan on crashing a The Death of the Death of Longform Journalism. Evan Ratliff of The Atavist and Max Linsky of Longform.org will talk about the comeback of longform writing on the web.
  • And last but not least, at 3:30pm on Sunday we'll be debuting the next Arc90 Lab experiment: Donahue. The talk, entitled Toss the Projector: Redefining the Presenter/Audience Dynamic explores new and innovative ways for audience and presenter to interact. Built in partnership with Behavior Design, and presented by Behavior's Chris Fahey and Arc90's Tim Meaney, we hope it will be a unique and provocative experience.
Beyond all the panels and presentations, we'd love to meet you if you're attending. On Sunday night, we plan on posting a meeting place on Twitter for a drink (or two). Be sure to follow us on Twitter for updates. See you there!
