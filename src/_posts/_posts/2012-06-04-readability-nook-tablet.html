--- layout: post title: Readability Now Available for the Nook Tablet categories: - News tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: "5" --- Since the early days, we've been fans of the Barnes & Noble NOOK devices. From the NOOK Simple Touch eReaders to the Android-powered NOOK tablet, Barnes & Noble has delivered a great family of devices for reading. Today, we're excited to announce the availability of Readability for the NOOK Tablet. It's a free app that brings you the great reading experience everyone's come to expect from Readability.You can access your reading list and read your saved pages even when you're offline. The app brings gorgeous typography and a wide array of styling options to bring an amazing reading experience to your NOOK Tablet. We're excited to have Readability available for the NOOK Tablet. It's great to bring together a great reading app with a great reading device. If you own a NOOK Tablet, there's no reason to wait. Grab Readability today.
