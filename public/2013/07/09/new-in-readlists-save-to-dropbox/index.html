New in Readlists: Save to Dropbox

by Tyler Gaw 25 May 2013 8 comments
Earlier today Dropbox announced a new and easy way for apps to save files to your Dropbox. We thought that sounded pretty great, so starting right now you can save any Readlist to your Dropbox with just a couple clicks. Save to Dropbox is located in the export section of every Readlist. All you'll need is a Dropbox account and a desire to collect some wonderfully curated content. If you're looking for excellent Readlists, check out the featured ones on the home page.
